# 📊 Contoso Retail — Product Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This page focuses on product-level performance, analysing how individual products contribute to revenue, profit, and overall business efficiency. It highlights both top-performing and underperforming products to support better pricing, inventory, and strategic decisions.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of This Page

The Product Analysis answers the following business questions:

- Which products generate the most revenue?  
- Which products generate the most profit?  
- Are high-selling products actually profitable?  
- Which products are underperforming or destroying profit?  
- How do sales, profit, and demand (orders) relate across products?  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (Top KPIs)

- **Total Sales** → Overall revenue generated  
- **Total Profit** → Net profit after costs  
- **Total Orders** → Total number of transactions  
- **Profit Margin %** → Overall profitability efficiency  

These KPIs provide a high-level view of product performance and profitability.

---

## 🥇 Top Products Analysis

### 🔹 Top 10 Products by Sales
- Ranks products based on total revenue  
- Identifies best-selling products  
- Highlights key revenue drivers  

### 🔹 Top 10 Products by Profit
- Ranks products based on profitability  
- Shows which products contribute most to profit  
- Allows comparison with top-selling products  

📌 **Insight:**  
- Many top-selling products are also highly profitable  
- Indicates a generally healthy pricing and cost structure  

---

## ⚠️ Bottom Products Analysis

### 🔻 Bottom 10 Products by Profit
- Identifies lowest-performing products  
- Highlights products with weak profitability  
- Helps detect potential loss-making or inefficient items  

📌 **Insight:**  
- Some products generate very low profit despite being sold  
- These may require pricing review, cost reduction, or discontinuation  

---

## 📈 Sales vs Profit Relationship

### 🔹 Scatter Plot: Sales vs Profit by Product
- **X-axis:** Total Sales  
- **Y-axis:** Total Profit  
- **Size:** Order Count  
- **Each point:** One product  

📌 **Key Insights:**  
- Strong positive relationship between sales and profit  
  → Higher sales generally lead to higher profit  
- A few products fall below the trend line  
  → High sales but relatively lower profit (low-margin products)  
- Some products show decent profit despite low sales  
  → Potential niche or high-margin opportunities  

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- **High sales ≠ always high profit**  
  → Some high-revenue products are less efficient  
- **Most top-selling products are profitable**  
  → Indicates strong overall business performance  
- **Low-profit products highlight inefficiencies**  
  → Opportunities for pricing or cost optimisation  
- **Product performance varies significantly**  
  → Requires targeted, product-level decision-making  

---

## 🚀 Business Value

This page enables stakeholders to:
- Focus on high-value products  
- Identify and fix low-margin items  
- Improve pricing and cost strategies  
- Optimise product portfolio for profitability  
- Make data-driven decisions at product level
