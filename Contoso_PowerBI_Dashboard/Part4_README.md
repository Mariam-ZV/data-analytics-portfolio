# 🌍 Contoso Retail — Geography Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This page analyses business performance across geographic regions. It focuses on where customers are located, how revenue is distributed globally, and which regions contribute most to overall performance.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of This Page

This page answers the following business questions:

- Where are our customers located globally?  
- Which countries generate the most revenue?  
- How are sales, orders, and customers distributed across regions?  
- How does performance change when focusing on specific continents?  

---

## 🗺️ Map — Sales by Country

**Visual: Bubble Map (Sales by Country)**  
- **Location:** Country  
- **Bubble Size:** Total Sales  
- **Tooltip:** Orders, Customers  

This visual shows the geographic distribution of revenue, highlighting dominant markets and regional gaps.

---

## 🎛️ Continent Slicer (Interactive Filter)

A horizontal slicer allows filtering the entire page by continent:

- Select All  
- Europe  
- North America  
- Pacific  

This enables focused regional analysis and comparison across markets.

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- **United States dominates total sales**  
  → Primary revenue driver  

- **Europe shows moderate but distributed performance**  
  → Key contributors include UK, Germany, France, and Netherlands  

- **Australia contributes smaller but meaningful revenue**  
  → Secondary but relevant market  

- **Customer and order distribution follow revenue patterns**  
  → Strong alignment between customer base and sales  

- **Performance varies significantly by continent**  
  → Business dependence differs across regions  

---

## 💡 Analytical Value

This page enables stakeholders to:

- Identify high-performing markets  
- Detect regional dependencies  
- Compare performance across continents  
- Support strategic decisions (expansion, marketing, resource allocation)  

---

## 🧠 Summary

The Geography page provides a clear, interactive view of where business value is generated globally. It allows quick identification of key markets and supports deeper exploration of regional performance.
